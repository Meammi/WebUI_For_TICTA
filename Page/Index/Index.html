Inspiration

Manga is a style of Japanese comic books and graphic novels, typically aimed at adults as well as children. With its unique storytelling and visually compelling illustrations, manga's linework is a defining characteristic for the reader’s engagement. However, there are challenges in Transitioning to Full Color, such as the labor-intensive manual, disruptions to artists’ workflow and the lack of automated colorization tools for consistency.With that in mind, we aim for the following:A completely automated colorization tool.Preserving original artist's intent while enhancing visual engagement.Ensuring consistent style and tone across multiple pages.
